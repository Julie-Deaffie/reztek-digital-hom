# Digital Muskeg: Theme & Style Guide

## 1. Core Philosophy
**"Ancient Roots, Digital Future"**

Digital Muskeg represents the intersection where the organic, chaotic, and ancient landscape of the muskeg meets the structured, precise, and rapid world of digital technology. It is a visual language that honors the land while embracing the code.

## 2. Color Palette

### The Ancient (Organic)
Colors derived from the Northern Manitoba landscape.
- **Muskeg Black**: `#1A1918` (Deep, rich soil)
- **Lichen Grey**: `#8C8A86` (Stone and dry moss)
- **Spruce Needle**: `#2C3E2E` (Deep forest green - accent)
- **Raw Bone**: `#EBE7DE` (Natural canvas background)

### The Modern (Digital)
Colors derived from technology and warning signals.
- **Signal Red**: `#EF4444` (The primary brand accent - Alert/Action)
- **Terminal Green**: `#00FF41` (Matrix/Code - subtle highlights)
- **Glitch Cyan**: `#00FFFF` (Digital distortion - rare accent)

## 3. Typography

### Headings: The Voice of Elders
**Font**: Serif / Display (e.g., *Playfair Display* or similar high-contrast serif)
- Represents tradition, history, and storytelling.
- Usage: Large headlines, philosophical statements.

### Data & UI: The Voice of the Machine
**Font**: Monospace (e.g., *JetBrains Mono*, *Fira Code*)
- Represents logic, structure, and code.
- Usage: Meta-data, dates, navigation, technical specs.

## 4. Visual Elements & Textures

### Topography x Circuitry
- **Concept**: Contour lines of a map that morph into printed circuit board (PCB) traces.
- **Usage**: Background patterns, dividers, and overlays.

### Organic Noise
- **Concept**: Film grain or paper texture.
- **Usage**: Overlays on clean digital elements to "ground" them in reality.

### The "Glitch" in the Wild
- **Concept**: Subtle chromatic aberration or pixel sorting on natural images.
- **Usage**: Hover states on images, transition effects.

## 5. UI Components

### Cards (The "Artifact")
- **Border**: Thin, technical lines (`1px solid #1A1918`).
- **Background**: Raw Bone (`#EBE7DE`) or Muskeg Black (`#1A1918`).
- **Interaction**: Sharp, instant transitions (no soft fades).

### Buttons (The "Trigger")
- **Style**: Brutalist, rectangular, uppercase.
- **Font**: Monospace.
- **Hover**: Invert colors or "glitch" shift.
